Juice'Em

My Final Year Project(FYP) at Nanyang Polytechnic. It is a VR game developed in Unity for the YAW VR.

This is a Oculus VR game about playing as a hamster in a high-tech hamster ball. The goal is to knock out opponent hamster balls in a battle royale arena, using your thrusters and grappling hook. There are also fruits in the level which can be juiced by running over them, and your hamster ball is fueled by this juice.

This game is multiplayer (Online) and designed to be showcased at school exhibitions, played using the YAW VR machine.

In this project, I developed most of the features, such as Networking, Unity ECS Boids (for the fruits), Particles, and overall gameplay and game state.
