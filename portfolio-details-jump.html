New Jumping Game

A game made in less than 24h for the GMTK Game Jam 2020.

This game was made to follow the theme of 'Out of Control'. The jump in this game gets out of control, in that the jump gets higher and higher. This results in interesting options for level design which I have explored in this short game. The main concept is to make the player aware of when to jump and when to NOT jump, thus turning a simple platformer into a puzzle-platformer.

I am the sole programmer for this project, and had help with asset creation by a friend.
